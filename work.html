Nomads

A travel diary app

Introduction

Summary

This was my Final Thesis project for the Academy of Art University's Web Design Master program. Nomads is a responsive website for travelers and travel lovers to share their experiences and get inspired for their next trip. Nomads allows people create travel memories by adding photos, notes, locations and tags and store them in one place and from these get automatic statistics.

Why?

I love traveling myself and "travel" through my friend's trips. Many travel diary apps are very one-side, but I wanted to focused on the social aspect more.

 

The challenge

Displaying travel experiences in a visually compelling and user-friendly way. Be innovative without compromising usability.

Conclusions

Nomads was a project evolved drastically over time. From concept, to research, to customer interviews, mockups, live prototypes, user testing and coding, this project helped me perfect different design skills.

UX Research

Secondary Research

I conducted initially secondary research by sending questionnaires to people and discover their needs when they travel. 56% of them mention travelling more than one time per year. Also, 86% percent mention traveling with friends or family. Thus, I realized the potencial of exploring a collaborative travel diary.

 

Primary Research

To see travelers in action, I conducted a field research at Pier 39, a touristic destination in San Francisco. I interviewed 6 people. All of them where within my target audience parameters - people between 20 and 40 traveling with friends or family - and all agree they would benefit from having an application to collect memories of their trip along with the people they travel with.
