BikeMark App

A bike app prototype.

Concept development

The motivation.

The main goal of this project was to explore ways of including motion into a mobile app. For this goal, I used Framer.JS library and HTML/CSS.

 

The Project

BikeMark is an app that encourages people to bike more by offering a social integration and having leaderboard and achievements. The ultimate goal is people to stay fit and happy because of the bike exercise.
