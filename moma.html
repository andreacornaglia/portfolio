Moma Audioguide

A JQuery Mobile App

Concept development

The motivation.

Visiting a museum can sometimes be a nightmare: lots of things to see in a small period of time, and usually a lot of information to be absorbed. Usually, people end up just looking at the art works without even knowing what is all about. Some of them offer an audioguide, which is an alternative, but it is extra work for visitors to find the place, sometimes make lines and wait for the device. In my last visit to a museum, the audioguide was simply an iphone that had a museum app. And given that nowadays almost every person owns a smartphone, why not just spare all the work and let the users install the app directly on their phones and use it as guides?

 

The Solution

A mobile app using JQuery Mobile that allows people to find artwork by searching or by scanning a QR code, the photo of the artwork and the audioguide complemented by a transcript.

Technology

JQuery Mobile

JQuery Mobile is a JQuery library which offers different functionalities to create a mobile website. Also used CSS3 for the design.

 

JSON

Because of the amount of information that goes into the audiobook (more than 10 artworks with audio and photo), I used JSON to create the pages dynamically. This helped to minimize the work and errors, and it is an scalable solution.
